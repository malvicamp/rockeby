About

About Elena

When she was in school, Elena Genova loved to admire some of her classmates' handwritings and would spend hours practising to replicate them. She then discovered her passion for graphic design and typography. Growing up in the USSR, she didn't have many opportunities for pursuing a career in design. However, she persisted and started first designing icons, followed by her first typeface: Handy Sans Condensed. The script Veryberry was her second one and reintroduced her to the pleasures of handwritten type and the freedom associated with it.

About Rockeby

Rockeby was issued on Feb 16, 2017 and is primarily a display face, but can be used for small amounts of body copy. The family contains a condensed sans-serif and a matching script, which can be used harmoniously together. It was originally inspired by the retro typeface Block Berthold (H. Hoffmann, 1908) and the more modern Din Condensed (Tagir Safayev, 1997). Its script companion is based on handwriting, as Elena has a love for beautifully written words.
